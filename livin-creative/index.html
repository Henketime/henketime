Who we are

Art is discovery

LIVIN Creative began somewhere between paint splatters and sketchbooks; late-night ideas and project lists; and the simple magic of making something with our hands.

We’re Liz and Kevin- lifelong creative explorers. Together, we built LIVIN Creative as a space where creativity can feel playful instead of pressured, inspiring instead of intimidating, and imperfect in all the best ways.

Here, you’ll find adult craft kits, creative workshops, and hands-on experiences designed to help you reconnect with your own creative rhythm. From time to time, you’ll also find our handmade creations.

We celebrate the many ways creativity shows up in our lives. That’s why we have kits for any occasion:

  • Creative reset for one
  • Creative adventure for two
  • Craft night in with friends
  • Gentle nudge toward self-inspiration

You found us, and we can’t wait to make things alongside you!

Thank you for being part of this growing creative community.
