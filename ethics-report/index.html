How to Verify Local Impacts

Ask these questions about your specific deployment:

  • Which cloud/region powers this LLM? (AWS us-east-1? Azure central-us?)
  • What’s the water source at that campus? (Potable? Reclaimed?)
  • Are site permits public? (Search [EPA ECHO](https://echo.epa.gov/) or state env agency for NPDES/SPCC.)
  • What’s the backup fuel? (Diesel → SPCC spill risk; HVO/biofuel → lower.)
Groundwater basics: Every major campus must have stormwater controls (SWPPP) and spill prevention (SPCC). But actual risk depends on local geology, chemicals used, and enforcement.
