Cross-Country Metal Circuits

Atlantic Spine

Furnace Crown + Worn Halo

Runs through Thunder Vault, Hollow Beacon Theater, and Rustline Depot. Best for fans chasing melodic chaos with high-speed pit transitions.

Heartland Grind

Ash Harbor + Iron Scripture

Moves across Static Forge Arena, Granite Howl Pavilion, and Mercy Coil Club. Expect tight stage handoffs and fast set turnovers.

Pacific Impact

Lands at Black Rail Hall, Smokestack Coliseum, and Last Echo Yard. Big room sound, thick bass, and wide mosh lanes.

Fan Priorities Before You Pick A Route

For Pit Runners

Prioritize venues with broad floor layouts and side exits so you can reset fast between sets.

For Tone Hunters

Chase rooms with concrete-and-steel shells that keep low frequencies aggressive but controlled.

For Barrier Viewers

Smaller halls like Rustline Depot deliver direct vocals and clearer rhythm detail if you lock in near center stage.
