Venue Radar

Know the room before the first riff drops.

Every venue below is fictional, but each profile is written to help real fans pick the right kind of night: brutal pit traffic, cleaner progressive mixes, or chest-rattle volume walls.

Top Fictional Rooms

Iron Cathedral

Tall steel trusses and hard reflective surfaces make this room ideal for bands with dense rhythm layers. The center floor stays active from open to close.

Rustline Depot

Former freight hall with a low stage and almost no distance between performers and crowd. Perfect for high-contact hardcore-adjacent sets.

Smokestack Coliseum

Wide bowl layout, huge PA arrays, and enough breathing room for giant pits while keeping sightlines clean from every angle.

Black Rail Hall

Balanced acoustic treatment with warm vocal presence. Best pick for bands that swing from atmospheric intros to full-speed blast sections.

Static Forge Arena

Industrial shell and angled crowd barriers. Great for elaborate stage setups and synchronized light rigs.

Thunder Vault

Concrete cavern feel with aggressive low-end retention. Bring earplugs and get ready for relentless kick drum impact.
