Some months ago, Kevin and I started talking and dreaming about our next adventure: a creative space to offer classes and workshops, a variety of art supplies, and feature local artists' goods. All while building a place to casually meet up with your local community, owned and held by us. We've decided to start by offering curated craft kits at local markets and online, and hosting events at local venues to build our brand while we work on a space. We are very excited!

Kevin and I have spent the last few weeks brainstorming possible names for our adventure ahead of our kit product launch. As you might imagine, for two creative strong-willed people this was nothing short of comical and exhaustive. We have narrowed it down to our favorites and are asking our family and friends to give us some feedback. Each name has its own meaning to us, but we don't want to sway anyone one way or the other. So, Kevin has created a rank choice voting system for you to give us your thoughts.

We will close voting this Friday, April 3rd. We will, of course, announce what we go with once we sort through it all.

Thank you all in advance for helping us bring this next adventure to life!
